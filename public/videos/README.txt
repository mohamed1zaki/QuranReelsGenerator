# /public/videos/

Drop your .mp4 background videos here.

Required filenames (edit lib/data.ts → BG_VIDEOS to change):
  rain.mp4
  stars.mp4
  ocean.mp4
  kaba.mp4
  apocalypse.mp4
  desert.mp4

Recommended:
  - Format: MP4 H.264 (most compatible)
  - Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical (1080x1920) — or any, will be cropped to fill
  - Duration: 15–60s (loops automatically)
  - Size: keep under 30MB per file for fast load

Free sources:
  https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/rain%20night/
  https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/starry%20sky/
  https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/ocean%20waves%20night/
